2️⃣ Low Arousal Threshold (Wakes too easily)

(You already approved these—now packaged for the PALM screener.)

✔ Patient-Core Question

“Are you a very light sleeper who wakes up easily during the night?”

✔ Supporting

“Do small breathing changes or noises wake you up?”
“Do you wake up tired even when your sleep apnea isn’t very bad?”
“Does a breathing machine make you wake up more instead of sleep better?”

3️⃣ High Loop Gain (Over-reactive breathing control / unstable breathing)

This is the hardest endotype to detect without physiology testing, but patient-level correlates exist.

✔ Patient-Core Question

“Do you feel like your breathing at night goes in waves — breathing too much, then too little?”

✔ Supporting

“Do you sometimes wake up suddenly feeling like you took a big gasp of air?”
“Do you have nights where your breathing feels ‘up and down’ rather than steady?”
“Has anyone said you breathe fast, then slow, during sleep?”

Clinician Correlates

Central apnea tendency

CO₂ sensitivity

CPAP intolerance due to over-ventilation

Cheyne-Stokes–type instability (but milder in many OSA patients)

Waxing/waning airflow

Your tool will NOT diagnose—only flag patterns.

4️⃣ Poor Muscle Compensation (Weak pharyngeal muscle response)

Patients cannot perceive airflow changes, but they can perceive positional challenges, effort, and tongue behaviors.

✔ Patient-Core Question

“Does your tongue fall back or block your throat when you lie on your back?”

✔ Supporting

“Do you snore much more when you sleep on your back?”
“Do you feel like your throat muscles get tired or weak at night?”
“Do you wake up choking or gasping when rolled onto your back?”

Clinician Correlates

High neuromuscular gain required to maintain patency

Positional OSA

Large tongue base (Zone 3)

Mandible/tongue crowding + weak compensatory reflexes

export function deriveEndotypes(answers: EndotypeAnswers) {
  return {
    pcritHigh: answers.totalZoneScore >= 4 || answers.anyZone3Plus,

    arousalThresholdLow:
      (answers.lightSleeper ? 1 : 0) +
        (answers.wakesFromSmallBreathingChanges ? 1 : 0) +
        (answers.unrefreshingDespiteMildOSA ? 1 : 0) +
        (answers.papMakesSleepLighter ? 1 : 0) >= 2,

    loopGainHigh:
      (answers.breathingWaves ? 1 : 0) +
        (answers.suddenGaspAwakenings ? 1 : 0) +
        (answers.upDownBreathingPattern ? 1 : 0) +
        (answers.fastThenSlowBreathingReported ? 1 : 0) >= 2,

    muscleCompensationPoor:
      (answers.tongueFallsBack ? 1 : 0) +
        (answers.positionalWorseOnBack ? 1 : 0) +
        (answers.throatMusclesFeelWeak ? 1 : 0) +
        (answers.chokingSupine ? 1 : 0) >= 2,
  };
}
